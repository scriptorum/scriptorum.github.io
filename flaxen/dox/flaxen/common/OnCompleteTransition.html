enum flaxen.common.OnCompleteTransition

Available on all platforms

When a transition "completes," what action should we take? This enum is a custom variation of flaxen.common.OnComplete. Currently, DestroyEntity is not supported.

Constructors

DestroyComponent

Destroy the component, removing it from the entity

None

Stop, do nothing, have a sandwich or something
